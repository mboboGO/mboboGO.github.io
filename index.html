Biography

I obtained my Ph.D. degree in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Maryland, College Park in 2018. My research advisor is Prof. Larry S. Davis. I was also affiliated with the University of Maryland Institute for Advanced Computer Studies (UMIACS). I obtained my Bachelor's degree from Tsinghua University in 2012, where I worked with Prof. Jiansheng Chen, Prof. Guangda Su and Prof. Yuantao Gu. I work on computer vision and pattern recognition problems with a focus on fine-grained object categorization during my Ph.D.

Update: I have left UMD but will regularly update this page for academic interests. Please feel free to contact me for anything you found interesting here :)
